The Best Movies According To Soumya!!

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time.

Titanic

My favourite romantic movie of all time. A must watch.Few learnings from it includes:-

  • Embrace life: Life is a gift, and you should be happy and embrace it gracefully.
  • Make every day count: Life is precious, and you should make every thought, word, and
    action count.

Spirited Away

Spirited Away is my favourite anime film that explores the themes of self-discovery, resilience,
and the importance of embracing change.Never be motivated by your greed.

Kiki's Delivery Service

Kiki's Delivery Service reminds viewers that they have to take care of themselves, trust their
friends, and understand that failure is natural.
